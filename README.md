# Premium E-Commerce Market Expansion: Strategic Data Analysis
### Data Analyst Case Study | WBS Coding School

## 🎯 Project Overview
Eniac, a European premium e-commerce brand specializing in high-end Apple accessories, is evaluating a rapid 1-year expansion into the Brazilian market. To overcome local logistical and bureaucratic hurdles, the board is considering a 3-year partnership with Magist, a local SaaS provider integrated with public postal services. This project analyzes a snapshot of Magist's database to determine if their marketplace environment matches Eniac's premium technology standards and if their delivery infrastructure is fast and reliable enough to protect our brand reputation.

---

## 📊 Dataset & Sources
* **Source:** [Magist Public Database Snapshot] (Simulated business case based on Olist public e-commerce data)
* **Dimensions:** 9 core relational tables containing ~112,650 global orders and 3,095 active sellers spanning a 25-month historical timeline.
* **Key Variables Used:** `price`, `product_category_name`, `order_purchase_timestamp`, `order_delivered_customer_date`, `order_estimated_delivery_date`, `product_weight_g`.
* **Data Pre-processing & Limitations:** 
  * Kept only completed deliveries (order_status = 'delivered') and excluded cancelled or unavailable orders to ensure lead time accuracy.
  * Filtered out null values in delivery timestamp metrics (`IS NOT NULL`) where date calculations were mathematically impossible.
  * All financial figures are strictly calculated and stored in **Brazilian Real (BRL / R$)**.

---

## 🚀 Key Business Insights & Findings
* **Strong Tech Consumer Base:** Tech categories represent **20.83% of total items sold** (23,461 units), proving that the platform has an established electronics market.
* **Premium Price Appetite:** High-end tech products (>150 BRL, defined via the 75th percentile) drive **24.98% of all tech volumes** and command a massive **68.06% share of total tech revenue**.
* **Specialized Storefronts:** **38.1% of platform revenue** is generated by tech merchants, and **60.5%** of them are specialized "pure tech" sellers.
* **The High-End Logistics Bottleneck:** While the global platform average delivery lead time is **12.5 days** (with a 93.1% on-time rate), shipping delays increase up to **8.58% specifically for Expensive Tech products**.
* **The Weight Constraint:** Delayed packages are **19% heavier** on average (2,452g vs 2,064g), indicating a clear transit bottleneck for heavy hardware components.
* **Operational Turnaround:** Historical trend analysis reveals a severe **Holiday Peak Crisis (Feb '18)** with delivery times spiking to **~17 days** due to Black Friday backlogs. However, by **August 2018**, Magist successfully optimized its network, hitting a record low delivery average of **<8 days**.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Recommendation
**RECOMMENDATION: SIGN THE 3-YEAR CONTRACT WITH CONDITIONAL SAFEGUARDS.**
Magist is a mature tech ecosystem capable of scaling. However, Eniac must enforce strict seasonal Service Level Agreements (SLAs) with financial penalties for the November–February peak season. Additionally, Eniac should utilize Magist's postal contracts for light accessories while auditing private airfreight options for heavy, high-ticket hardware bulk shipments.
